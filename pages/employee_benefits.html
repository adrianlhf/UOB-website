Main Coverage Types

Group Term Life (GTL) - Covers Death and Total & Permanent Disability (TPD) due to causes such as illnesses or accidents.

Group Hospital & Surgical (GHS) - Covers medical expenses incurred as a result of hospitalisation or surgery due to illness or accident.

Group Personal Accident - Covers Death, TPD and Dismemberment resulting from accidental injuries only.
You can also add up to seven different supplementary riders to provide a more comprehensive customised solution for your business.
