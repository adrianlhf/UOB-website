Promotion

Earn double cash bonus of up to $1,288 when you open a business account online, deposit fresh funds and make qualifying transactions.

How it works:

Step 1:

Open a UOB Business Account by 30 September 2020

Step 2:

Earn cash bonus when you top up and maintain fresh funds of at least $30,000

- The first incremental $30,000 top up will earn you $25 cash bonus

- Subsequently, every incremental $50,000 top up will earn you $25 cash bonus

Step 3:

Double your cash bonus to $50 for each incremental top up tier when you perform any 2 of the following qualifying transactions:

- Book a FX contract of at least S$20,000 (or its foreign currency equivalent)

- Any GIRO payment or collection

- At least 2 OTT/ITT transactions

- Incoming or outgoing PayNow Corporate transactions of at least S$10,000

New UOB Customers will receive an additional welcome cash bonus of $38!

Here is an example of how much cash bonus you will receive if you are new customer of UOB and open an account with us from 15 July to 30 September 2020.

If you top up $180,000 and consistently maintain the top-up account balance:

First $30,000 top up = $25
Remaining $150,000 top up = ($150,000/$50,000) x $25 = $75
Top-up cash bonus earned = $100

Your cash bonus will be doubled when you make 2 qualifying transactions:

First $30,000 top up = $50
Remaining $150,000 top up = ($150,000/$50,000) x $50= $150
Top-up cash bonus earned = $200

Welcome cash bonus for new UOB customers = $38

Total cash bonus earned = $238

For example, for payments to be made on October 15 for Giro Transfer or Payroll Request, the Value Date is October 14 i.e. one (1) working day prior to the date when payees' receiving accounts will receive the funds. On October 14, the receiving accounts are credited at the Bank's end-of-day processing (which is around midnight). The receiving accounts can use the funds on October 15. Deposit Insurance Scheme
Singapore dollar deposits of non-bank depositors and monies and deposits denominated in Singapore dollars under the Supplementary Retirement Scheme are insured by the Singapore Deposit Insurance Corporation, for up to S$75,000 in aggregate per depositor per Scheme member by law. Foreign currency deposits, dual currency investments, structured deposits and other investment products are not insured.

Please refer to UOB Insured Deposit Register for a list of UOB accounts / products that are covered under the Scheme.
