Introduction

BizCare is a comprehensive* business insurance that provides wide coverage against your risk exposures, so you can conduct your business with peace of mind.



Why choose BizCare?

  • Comprehensive* insurance coverage including cyber risks
  • Quick approval with no underwriting required
  • Fuss-free enrolment and renewal

Protect what matters in business

You have put your heart and soul into building your business so it is crucial to protect it. From guarding against unforeseen accidents or losses, to protecting your most important asset, your people.

What BizCare covers

Electronic Equipment

Covers cost of repairs to or replacement of electronic data processing equipment caused by any unforeseen and sudden physical loss or damage from any cause other than those specifically excluded

All Risks

Covers accidental physical loss of or damage on all Personal Properties including but not limited to contents, office equipment, stocks-in-trade, furniture, machinery and equipment and all other personal properties of every kind and description belonging to you or properties leased, rented, hired by you.

Plate Glass

idemnifies you for the breakage of fixed glass insured in your business premises.

Burglary

Covers physical loss of or damage to your insured property as a result of theft by forcible and violent entry/exit of your business premises.

Cyber Risks

Covers interruption of computer systems caused directly by a failure of computer security to prevent security breach

Public Liability

Covers legal liability for accidental bodily injury or property damage to third parties arising out of the operation of your business

Work Injury Compensation

Covers legal liability towards your employees for bodily injury sustained by accident or disease arising out of and in the course of their employment.

Increased Cost of Working

Covers in conjunction with All Risks. Daily benefit shall be payable (up to maximum 100 days) if the interruption of or interference with your business results in a total suspension from operation.

Forms of Money

Covers loss of or damage to money whilst kept within your business premises or whilst in transit anywhere in Singapore provided it is in your personal custody or custody of your authorised employees.

Fidelity Guarantee

Covers loss of money or property, belonging to you or for which you are legally responsible as a direct result of any act of fraud or dishonesty committed by your employees.
