Products & Services That Might Interest You

SME Micro Loan

Help local small businesses gain unsecured working capital financing under EFS. Offers loan up to S$100,000 to young businesses that meet this criteria

Business Property Loan

Provides financing option for SMEs looking to purchase industry/commercial property or refinancing their existing industry/commercial property loans with other banks

SME Enhanced Working Capital Loan

Help SMEs expand their business and gain access to working capital financing to support their day-to-day business operations.

BizMoney Loan

With a high loan quantum, and no collateral needed, you can easily apply for this loan to run and grow your business. Apply now to fund your expansion plans!

SME Equipment Loan

Looking to purchase new equipment? We can help finance up to 90% of the market valuation or purchase price of your new equipment.

Equipment & Vehicle Loan

Finances the purchase of new commercial vehicles, machinery and equipment. Obtain ownership of the asset now upon fulfilling payment of loan installment.

Temporary Bridging Loan

Improve your cash flow with this loan scheme announced at the Resilience and Solidarity Budget 2020, now expanded to include all businesses.
