Description

Search for your ideal commercial industrial properties with convenience and confidence, based on your bank loan eligibility. Or, get credible instant property valuation that you can get a UOB Business Property Loan for. Access to legal advice is also easy. The first and only platform that brings together all your commercial industrial property purchase needs.

Real Commercial - 4 key features for business owners/companies to search/buy with confidence and convenience.

1. Standardized commercial industrial listings in an easy-to-read format that is accurate and up-to-date.

2. Purchase within your budget using UOB's Loan Eligibility Assessment.

3. Ascertain market value with bank-backed property valuation.

4. Get access to a lawyer for all your legal enquiries.
